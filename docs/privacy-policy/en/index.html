Principle: your data stays on your device

Trainist works without a user account and without its own server. Everything you enter — food entries, weight values, goals, custom foods, exercises, workout templates and sessions — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. We do not collect this data, transmit it to any server, or share it with third parties.

Network access

  • Barcode scanning (food search): When you scan or manually enter a barcode, the app sends the barcode number to the open, non-profit database Open Food Facts to look up product data (name, nutritional values). For technical reasons your IP address is transmitted to Open Food Facts in the process; no further personal data is sent along with it. The Open Food Facts privacy policy applies to this request.
  • Fonts: The app downloads its fonts (Newsreader, Hanken Grotesk, JetBrains Mono) at runtime via Google Play Services' Google Fonts provider. This involves the usual technical requests to Google; no app content or personal data from Trainist is transmitted.

The app does not include any tracking, advertising network, or analytics service.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used exclusively for scanning barcodes (on-device recognition via ML Kit). No images are stored or transmitted.
  • Notifications: For the daily reminder to log your meals and for the workout rest timer. Both run entirely locally on the device.
  • Exact alarms: So the workout rest timer reliably notifies you at the right time, even while the device is idle. Used solely for local timing — no data is collected.

Backup & data export

If you have enabled Android's automatic backup (Google account backup), Android backs up the local app database like any other app data — this runs through your own Google account and is subject to Google's own privacy policy, not ours.

The data export in Settings creates a ZIP file with your data, which you send yourself, via your device's share function, to a destination of your choice (e.g. your own cloud storage, a local file). We have no access to it.

Deletion

Individual entries can be deleted in the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data (unless separately preserved elsewhere via Android's own device backup).

Children

Trainist is not specifically directed at children under the age of 16.
